Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • Spices as per taste

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Sauté onions, add spices, then add tomatoes and cream. Add paneer and simmer until well combined.

Chole Bhature

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas, soaked and boiled
  • Spices as per taste
  • 2 cups flour
  • Oil for frying

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Cook chickpeas with spices. Knead dough, roll out, and fry for bhature. Serve hot.

Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Spices for biryani
  • 1/4 cup yogurt

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Layer rice and vegetables with spices. Cook on low heat until flavors meld.

Rajma Chawal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup kidney beans, soaked and boiled
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • Spices as per taste
  • 1 cup rice

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Cook kidney beans with spices and tomatoes. Serve with steamed rice.

Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • Spices as per taste
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • Salt to taste

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Sauté vegetables with spices, add rice and cook until done.
