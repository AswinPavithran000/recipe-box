Delicious Breakfast Dishes

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds. Grind into a smooth batter and ferment overnight. Pour batter on a hot griddle and cook with a bit of oil until crispy.

Poha

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • Salt and turmeric to taste

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Rinse poha and drain. In a pan, add mustard seeds, onions, and green chili. Add poha, turmeric, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.

Aloo Paratha

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt and spices to taste
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Mix flour with water to make dough. Stuff with spiced mashed potatoes, roll, and cook on a griddle with ghee.

Idli

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Soak rice and urad dal, then grind to make a batter. Ferment overnight and steam in idli molds until soft.
