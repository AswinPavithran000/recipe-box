Tasty Snacks & Sweets

Samosa

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt and spices to taste
  • Oil for frying

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Make dough with flour and water, fill with spiced potatoes, and deep-fry until golden brown.

Bhel Puri

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Sev for topping

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Mix puffed rice with onions, tomatoes, chutney, and top with sev. Serve immediately.

Kachori

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup gram flour
  • Spices for filling
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Prepare dough, fill with spiced gram flour, and deep-fry until crisp.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • Ghee for frying
  • Sugar syrup

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Form dough with milk powder, fry until golden, and soak in sugar syrup.

Pani Puri

Ingredients:

  • Puri shells
  • 1/2 cup boiled potatoes, mashed
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Spicy water
  • Chickpeas for filling

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Fill puri shells with potatoes, chickpeas, chutney, and spicy water. Serve immediately.
