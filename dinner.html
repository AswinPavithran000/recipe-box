Savory Dinner Dishes

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • Spices as per taste
  • Butter for cooking

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Cook chicken with spices and butter, add cream and simmer until thick.

Dal Tadka

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils (dal)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Garlic and spices to taste

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Cook dal with spices. Sauté garlic and add as tadka (tempering) on top before serving.

Fish Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish fillet
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • Spices as per taste

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Sauté onions, add spices, and tomatoes, then add fish. Simmer until fish is cooked.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, bell pepper, broccoli)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Stir-fry vegetables with soy sauce and ginger-garlic paste until tender.

Pasta Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Video:

Cooking Instructions:

Cook pasta, then mix with cream, cheese, salt, and pepper until creamy.
